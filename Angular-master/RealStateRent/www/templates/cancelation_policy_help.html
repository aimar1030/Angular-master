Cancelation Policy

Close

  • Without cancellation policy - the tenant is not charged any cancellation fees for the transaction at any stage, until the end of the rental date.

  • Flexible Cancellation Policy - tenant who canceled the transaction from the beginning of the transaction approval until 48 hours prior to rental - will be charged a cancellation fee of $ 0.3. Tenant who canceled the deal 48 hours before the rental will be charged a cancellation fee 0.3 $, 10% of the transaction value. Tenant who canceled the deal during the rental period will be charged a cancellation fee of $ 0.3, a 10% transaction fee for a number of days of 4 which the product was in renter's possession and two more days but, not more than the total rental days which were agreed upon with the lender.

  • Strict Cancellation Policy - renter will be fully charged with no possibility of refund, once the rental transaction is confirmed by the system.

  • In any case of cancellation, regardless of the cancellation policy agreed upon between renter and lender, the renter will be refunded after deduction of clearing fees and any additional fees due to the Company with respect to the transaction.
